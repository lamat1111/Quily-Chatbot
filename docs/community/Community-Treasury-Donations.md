## Can I Donate to Quily or Support the Quilibrium Project?

Quily (the chatbot) does not have a personal wallet and cannot accept tips or donations directly. However, the Quilibrium community runs a **Community Treasury** that you can contribute to if you'd like to support the ecosystem.

The treasury is a community-driven initiative that seeks to empower web3 enthusiasts, developers, and industry disruptors through strategic funding of community-led initiatives. Its mission is to expand Quilibrium's reach and foster innovation for a more secure, open, and equitable Internet.

Participation is fully optional and does not grant additional privileges within the Quilibrium network.

## What Does the Treasury Fund?

The treasury's initial priorities include:

- **Educational & marketing content** — producing premium content to grow awareness.
- **CEX listings & market making** — increasing accessibility of the QUIL token.
- **Developer grants** — onboarding builders into the Quilibrium ecosystem.
- **Contests & ambassador programs** — driving community engagement.

## How to Donate

The treasury accepts ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum only.

**ERC-20 Treasury Address:**
`0xE09e96E3A3CCBEafC0996d6c0214E10adFD01D65`
[View on Etherscan](https://etherscan.io/address/0xE09e96E3A3CCBEafC0996d6c0214E10adFD01D65)

**Accepted tokens:** ETH, USDT, USDC, wQUIL (must be on Ethereum).

**⚠️ Do NOT send native QUIL to this address.** Only ERC-20 tokens on Ethereum are accepted.

**⚠️ IMPORTANT — Always verify the address before sending funds!**
Before making any donation, please double-check the treasury address via the [official Discord announcement](https://discord.com/channels/1212446221395042335/1212447064861184060/1305995640714694729). Never rely solely on a chatbot or website for contract addresses — always verify on Discord to ensure the address has not been tampered with.

- **Peτer Jameson** — Node runner since Oct 2023, community founder

All contributions are used responsibly to support the project. Monthly transparency reports detailing how funds are spent are posted in the **#community-treasury** channel on Discord. Community input is encouraged there as well.

Major contributors will be given a voice in governance decisions (more details to follow).

Your support is greatly appreciated and will help the Quilibrium ecosystem thrive!
