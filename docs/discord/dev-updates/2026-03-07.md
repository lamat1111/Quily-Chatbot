# dev-updates - March 7, 2026

## Cassie — 8:09 AM UTC

**2.1**
Still seeing active join and leave requests in flight, going to update the dashboard to give some more helpful info for folks to see what the actual progress is as the summary numbers appear stagnant – should be also helpful for deciding future actions based on progress rate of shard out.

**QClient**
Going to prepare the qclient release, so node operators that want to take more direct action for their allocations are able to do so, i imagine this will become important in the coming weeks.

**QNS, Quorum Wallet, Q Service Billing**
QNS was given a UI update that had been pending, we're going to follow up with a backend update to move towards our metavm runner for ethereum and other chains, removing any dependency on external RPCs, also sets the stage for supporting solana for transactions. Quorum rpc proxy and QConsole's billing engine will also get moved to this, quicknode lied in their documentation about the breadth of their support and magically the "answer" to fix it is more money. Impact calculus is simple, our rpc costs us practically nothing, so we'll just switch to it.

**QConsole**
Next QConsole update will also unlock multi-user logins, including support to sign in with email+2fa, which have been the most common feature requests by dev teams using it. We'll also roll out Klearu, F(x) and raw MetaVM support in a fast follow

**Quorum Mobile**
App store review on the apple side got back to us with a soft rejection, related to abuse reporting (not the easiest thing to support, but we'll forge a path forward), it's likely google will either make it through eventually or find something similar.

There's also some other large objectives in flight, but I'll leave them out of scope for this update recap.
